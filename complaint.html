  • (2) That Complainant purchased 4 computer set(HP All-in-one 24) from Shree Technologies (Certified seller HP) situated at Suraj Nagar, Manali for 215,000 Rupees which was paid by him via Debit card of State Bank Of India around 3:45 P.M, receipt with no. 389000 generated after swipe of card. The receipt of the transaction is attached as well.


    Annexure 1 – Receipt of Debit card Of SBI
  • (3) That, after few days of purchasing of All-in-one 24 (4 Sets) by complainant, 2 computers got countered with several problems that are -

    • Lacking and slowing down while working
    • Late response of the screen
    • Several camera related problem
    That these problems are very serious defects and creating disturbance in workingof complainant, and according to the terms and conditions mentioned in waruntee card the respondent is liable to fix all the defects.
  • (4) That, the computer sets are under waruntee period which is provided by the HP company that is 180 days, so when the complainant countered with these problem he visited the shop and requested to respondent on dated 28 May 2021 to fix the defects of the Computer sets but the owner of Shree Technologies had denied to do so and also refuses to register any grivenace of the complainant in HP company grivenance cell, which is the breach of policy of waruntee of 180 days given by the HP Private India Limited.

    • Waruntee papers of Computer Sets (Annexure 2)
    • Purchase receipt of Shop
  • The price of the mobile of that is All-in-one 24 computer sets are below 20 lacks so, this forum has jurisdiction to determine and adjudicate this dispute.
  • That the present complaint is being filed within the period prescribed under section 24A of the Act.
  • That the respondent have not only refused to fix the defects of the computer sets but also violate the company’s policy by refusing to fix defects and registering the grievenance of the complainant when approached to Shree Technologies, hence liable to refund the price of Computer Sets and also compensate towards financial loss, physical inconvience suffered by complainant on account of breach of contract, breach of waruntee, breach of duty and breach of trust on the part of respondent for which complainant claims sum of rupees that is

    Refund Amount of Computers 2,15,000
    Physical Inconvinices and Financial loss   50,000
